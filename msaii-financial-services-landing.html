Compliance & Trust

Built For Regulated Industries, Not Around Them

Every system runs on a human-in-the-loop principle. AI prepares; a licensed professional approves. These guardrails are engineered in from day one — they're a differentiator, not a disclaimer.

Human-in-the-Loop

AI drafts documents, reports, briefs, and outreach. A licensed CPA, adviser, or producer approves every coverage, suitability, tax, or filing decision. AI never binds, advises, or files on its own.

SEC / IAA Alignment

Off-channel communications are captured and archived. Client-facing AI content is routed through compliance review before it's ever sent.

TCPA Consent

Prior express written consent is captured before any automated text or call, with a clear opt-out — confirmed against your E&O carrier before activation.

NAIC Model Bulletin

AI use by insurance agencies is documented with governance, testing, and oversight aligned to the 2023 NAIC Model Bulletin on AI.

GLBA Data Security

PII is handled under strict access controls and vendor-management discipline — positioned as part of the value delivered, not a liability carried.

Full Audit Trail

Every automated action is logged for books-and-records and exam readiness — turning automation into a compliance asset rather than a new risk.
